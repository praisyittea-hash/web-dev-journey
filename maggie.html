How to make Maggie

Maggie is chinese style noodles from meesho

Ingredients

  • maggie packet
  • veggies
  • maggie masala
  • water

Instructions

  1. add water to bowl on stove and turn it on
  2. put the dry maggie into the bowl
  3. add all the veggies
  4. after 7-8 mins add maggie masala and mix
